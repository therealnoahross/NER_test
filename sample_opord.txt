OPERATION ORDER 001-25

1. SITUATION
   a. Enemy Forces: The 3rd Motorized Rifle Brigade is located at Grid AB 1234 5678. 
      Enemy strength is estimated at 2,000 personnel with T-72 tanks and BMP-2 vehicles.
   
   b. Friendly Forces: 1st Battalion, 37th Armor Regiment is positioned at FOB Thunder.
      2nd Brigade Combat Team provides support from the north.

2. MISSION
   1-37 AR attacks to seize Objective Falcon NLT 0600 hours on D-Day.

3. EXECUTION
   a. Commander's Intent: Destroy enemy forces in zone to enable follow-on operations.
   
   b. Concept of Operations:
      - Alpha Company attacks along Axis Red to Phase Line Blue
      - Bravo Company supports by fire from Hill 231
      - CPT Johnson commands Alpha Company
      - CPT Williams commands Bravo Company

4. SERVICE SUPPORT
   - Ammunition resupply at Logistics Point Charlie
   - Medical evacuation via Route Green to Field Hospital Delta

5. COMMAND AND SIGNAL
   - COL Smith commands 1-37 AR from TAC CP at Grid AB 1200 5600
   - Primary frequency: 45.50 MHz
